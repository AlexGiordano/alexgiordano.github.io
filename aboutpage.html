I have played a range of instruments over the years but am always drawn back to the guitar. 'Just when I thought I was out...'

I find it to be a technically formidable instrument. Unlike the orchestral stringed instruments, it has six strings which are not tuned in equal intervals, and straddles being both a melodic and chordal instrument, despite the constraint of using four fingers of the left hand. The mandolin is a fantastic instrument that is the focus of virtuosos like Chris Thile, Mike Marshall and Adam Steffey, and can also open up the classical or traditional repertoire of the fiddle to a guitarist.
